Hard P

Shortcut: P

Hard P is p-channel MOSFET with Vgs(th) = -3.0V. It means that it would open if Vg - Vs <= -3.0V. And it would really do if it were totally ideal. But Terilog tries not to forget completely about unideal nature of components. That's why you should feel like Hard P 'does not like' negative source voltages: if source is negative and gate is stable - the drain will go negative (regardless to Vg).
