Half Adder

Half Adder is an arithmetic circuit with two inputs (trit A, trit B) and two outputs (their Sum and Carry propagation). As it follows from their names, Half Adder takes input trits A and B, adds them together and outputs their sum and the carry appeared during addition. In ternary logic it is possible to construct Half Adder with the use of 14 MOSFETs. Of course, we mean Half Adder with negated outputs. But there is no dramatic difference between normal and negated Half Adders, because both of them can be used to make Full Adder.
